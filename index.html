About Me

Thanks for visiting my site. Let me tell you a little bit about me.

My name is Shaun. I work as a senior technical writer at a software company called Matillion. Before that, I worked as an SEO content writer at a business services company called Peninsula.

Before that, my first full-time job was as a digital marketing assistant at a recruitment agency called Worldwide Recruitment Solutions.

Educationally, I have a Master of Arts degree in creative writing from the University of Manchester, and a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Wales, Aberystwyth—that degree is in English and creative writing.

As you can see at this point, writing is basically what I spend most of my day doing in some form or another. I can't say I anticipated while doing my degrees that I would one day be writing software documentation for a living, but what I do know is:

  • When I got my first iPhone, a second-hand iPhone 4 that I bought from a friend for £80 in 2014, I became very enamoured of the concept of apps. There was no precocious, prodigal "ureka!"" moment, but what there was was a sudden interest in how user-friendly this device I'd bought was. I enjoyed downloading apps just to try them out. I found myself curious about updates, and became someone who refreshes the App Store app every day to keep my apps updated.
  • Reading and writing release notes is weirdly fun.
  • Technical writing means you get to work with a whole boat-load of very clever people.
  • It has led to me learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Swift. I consider myself intermediate in the first two, and a beginner in the other three. But one day...!
  • Technical writing pays pretty damn well.
